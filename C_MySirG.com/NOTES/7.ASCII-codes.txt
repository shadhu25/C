ASCII codes
    Americon Standard Code for Information Interchange

ASCII codes is the numerical representation of a character such as 'a' or '@' or an action of some sort.

ASCII was developed a long time ago and now the non-printing characters are rarely used for their original purpose.

ASCII was actually designed for use with teketypes
A teleprinter(teletyoewriter, Teletype or TTY) is an electomechnical tyoewriter that can be used to send and recieve typed messages from point point and point-to-muitipoint over various types of communications channels.
